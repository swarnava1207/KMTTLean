    annIdeal R a is the annihilating ideal of all p : R[X] such that p(a) = 0.

    The informal notation p(a) stand for Polynomial.aeval a p. Again informally, the annihilating ideal of a is { p ‚àà R[X] | p(a) = 0 }. This is an ideal in R[X]. The formal definition uses the kernel of the aeval map.
