    Relations between extend and restriction #

    Given an embedding e : Embedding c c' of complex shapes satisfying e.IsRelIff, we obtain a bijection e.homEquiv between the type of morphisms K ‚ü∂ L.extend e (with K : HomologicalComplex C c' and L : HomologicalComplex C c) and the subtype of morphisms œÜ : K.restriction e ‚ü∂ L which satisfy a certain condition e.HasLift œÜ.
