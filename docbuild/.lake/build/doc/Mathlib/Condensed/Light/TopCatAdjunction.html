    The adjunction between light condensed sets and topological spaces #

    This file defines the functor lightCondSetToTopCat : LightCondSet.{u} тед TopCat.{u} which is left adjoint to topCatToLightCondSet : TopCat.{u} тед LightCondSet.{u}. We prove that the counit is bijective (but not in general an isomorphism) and conclude that the right adjoint is faithful.

    The counit is an isomorphism for sequential spaces, and we conclude that the functor topCatToLightCondSet is fully faithful when restricted to sequential spaces.
