    The adjunction between condensed sets and topological spaces #

    This file defines the functor condensedSetToTopCat : CondensedSet.{u} тед TopCat.{u+1} which is left adjoint to topCatToCondensedSet : TopCat.{u+1} тед CondensedSet.{u}. We prove that the counit is bijective (but not in general an isomorphism) and conclude that the right adjoint is faithful.

    The counit is an isomorphism for compactly generated spaces, and we conclude that the functor topCatToCondensedSet is fully faithful when restricted to compactly generated spaces.
