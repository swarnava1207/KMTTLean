    The monoidal structure on the category of bialgebras #

    In Mathlib.RingTheory.Bialgebra.TensorProduct, given two R-bialgebras A, B, we define a bialgebra instance on A ‚äó[R] B as well as the tensor product of two BialgHoms as a BialgHom, and the associator and left/right unitors for bialgebras as BialgEquivs. In this file, we declare a MonoidalCategory instance on the category of bialgebras, with data fields given by the definitions in Mathlib.RingTheory.Bialgebra.TensorProduct, and Prop fields proved by pulling back the MonoidalCategory instance on the category of algebras, using Monoidal.induced.
