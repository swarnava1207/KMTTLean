    Harris-Kleitman inequality #

    This file proves the Harris-Kleitman inequality. This relates #𝒜 * #ℬ and 2 ^ card α * #(𝒜 ∩ ℬ) where 𝒜 and ℬ are upward- or downcard-closed finite families of finsets. This can be interpreted as saying that any two lower sets (resp. any two upper sets) correlate in the uniform measure.

    Main declarations #

    • IsLowerSet.le_card_inter_finset: One form of the Harris-Kleitman inequality.

    References #

    • [D. J. Kleitman, Families of non-disjoint subsets][kleitman1966]
