    Dot product of two vectors #

    This file contains some results on the map dotProduct, which maps two vectors v w : n → R to the sum of the entrywise products v i * w i.

    Main results #

    • dotProduct_stdBasis_one: the dot product of v with the ith standard basis vector is v i
    • dotProduct_eq_zero_iff: if v's dot product with all w is zero, then v is zero
