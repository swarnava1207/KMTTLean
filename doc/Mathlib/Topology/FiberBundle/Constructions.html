    Standard constructions on fiber bundles #

    This file contains several standard constructions on fiber bundles:

    • Bundle.Trivial.fiberBundle ùïú B F: the trivial fiber bundle with model fiber F over the base B

    • FiberBundle.prod: for fiber bundles E‚ÇÅ and E‚ÇÇ over a common base, a fiber bundle structure on their fiberwise product E‚ÇÅ √ó·µá E‚ÇÇ (the notation stands for fun x ‚Ü¶ E‚ÇÅ x √ó E‚ÇÇ x).

    • FiberBundle.pullback: for a fiber bundle E over B, a fiber bundle structure on its pullback f *·µñ E by a map f : B' ‚Üí B (the notation is a type synonym for E ‚àò f).

    fiber bundle, fibre bundle, fiberwise product, pullback
